Earthship Seattle | WordPress, Web Design

Overview

Earthship Seattle was a group design and development Website project at Seattle Central College in Autumn 2014.

Two designers enrolled in WEB 210: Advanced Web Design were paired with two developers enrolled in ITC 210: Advanced Web Development.

Although I was enrolled in WEB 210, I participated in both roles, as designer and developer.

Initial Client Meeting

In our initial meeting, the clients expressed that they wanted to replace their existing site.

The clients also gave us some insight to some of their goals, and how they intended for users to use the site.
