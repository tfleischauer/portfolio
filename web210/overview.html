Client: Earthship Seattle | WordPress, Web Design

Overview

This was a team project at Seattle Central College. Two students from Advanced Web Design were paired with two students from Advanced Web Development.

Each team was assigned to design and build a custom WordPress site for a client from a non-profit organization.

I was enrolled as a designer, but also participated in development of the site.

After the quarter ended, I continued work on the project and completed the site for internship credit in spring 2015.

Skills used: Illustrator, Photoshop, HTML, CSS, PHP, Responsive Design, WordPress, Komodo Edit, GitHub.
