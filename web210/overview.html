Earthship Seattle | WordPress, Web Design

Overview

This was a team project at Seattle Central College in Autumn 2014.

Two students from Advanced Web Design were paired with two students from Advanced Web Development.

Each team was assigned to build a custom WordPress site for a client from a non-profit organization.

I was enrolled as a designer, but also participated in development of the site.

Initial Client Meeting

Our team's client, Earthship Seattle, expressed that they wanted to replace their existing site.

The clients gave us insight to some of their goals, and how they intended for users to use the site.

Skills used: Illustrator, Photoshop, HTML, CSS, Responsive Design, WordPress, Komodo Edit, GitHub.
