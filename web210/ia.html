Information Architecture

After drawing up Project Proposal and Terms & Conditions contracts, the design team was responsible for determining the Information Architecture.

Based off notes from our initial client meeting, we started with a card sort.

We decided to try Google Drawings instead of using Illustrator or Photoshop to make an Information Architecture diagram.

Google Drawings allowed for the whole team to participate in diagramming, commenting, and also offered version control.

Eventually the team arrived at a solution, and after a few adjustments, our instructor approved the diagram.

We then discussed the proposed Information Architecture with the clients for their approval.
