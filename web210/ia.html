Information Architecture

After drawing up some contracts for the clients, the design team was responsible for determining the Information Architecture.

Based off notes from our client meeting, we started with a card sort.

We decided to try Google Drawings instead of using Illustrator or Photoshop to make an Information Architecture diagram.

Google Drawings allowed for the whole team to participate in diagramming or commenting, plus version control.

Eventually the team arrived at a solution. After a few adjustments, our instructor approved the diagram.

We then discussed the proposed Information Architecture with the clients for their approval.
