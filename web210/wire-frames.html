Wire Frames

In Illustrator, our team decided to make wire frames on a grid that matched our primary media queries: 480px, 800px, and 940px.

We eventually added two more media queries: 640px the mid-way point between 480 and 800, and 320px to account for minimum width / lowest resolution devices.

This wire frame collaboration gave us more experience with organizing our layer panels in Illustrator so that we could exchange files as we developed our concepts.
