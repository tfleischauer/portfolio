Welcome!

My name is Troy Fleischauer. I am a designer and information professional located in the Pacific Northwest.

I love being an advocate for any given system’s user.

Here you will see examples of my client and academic work in Human-Computer Interaction and Web design.

Process

I enjoy the process of making information systems usable by researching user needs to make artifacts such as:

  • Information Architecture Diagrams
  • Wire Frames
  • User Interfaces

I also enjoy producing Web content and working on team projects that involve:

  • Photoshop
  • Illustrator
  • Copywriting
  • Interaction Design
  • Prototyping

I live in the space between user objectives and business goals.
