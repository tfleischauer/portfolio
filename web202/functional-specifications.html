Functional Specifications

The purpose of the Functional Specification was to define the scope and requirements of the project.

As a Web Designer, during this hypothetical phase, I get a chance to state specifically what I intend to build. This allows the client to review and approve the project scope.

The Functional Specification also serves to prepare a set of requirements needed to meet the stated functional goals and project scope.

This Functional Specification is made as an academic exercise. In real life, the specifications would be much more detailed and specific.
