After determining the general site architecture, I made several design comps in Adobe Illustrator to express some variations of a theme that I wanted to develop for this project.

Design Concept

My overall concept for this site was to be as transparent as possible.

The paintings are so great, I just wanted to feature them and stay out of the way.

Hopefully, the user barely even notices the web design.
