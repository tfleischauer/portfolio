Audience, Issues, Approach

The first step in this project was to determine the primary audience that will use this site.

After an exploratory meeting with my client, we determined a few potential user types.

Based off these user types, I made three personas:

  • Adrian the Artist
  • Clarence the Clergy Member
  • Chris from the Community

These personas helped us to explored potential issues each of these users might have.

Anticipating user issues helped us to determine some approaches to fulfill our users' expectations and task-oriented goals.
