Design Ideas and Sketches

After our User Research and Persona creation, the team held meetings to show and discuss sketches and drawings.

Often the team meetings would include white boarding to aid us in our ideation and exploration of ways to solve the problem of bringing a sense of home to students living remotely.

The team would explore and discuss strengths, weaknesses, feasibility, originality, and whether the proposed solutions would fit the needs of our personas.
