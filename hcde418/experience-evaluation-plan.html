Experience Evaluation Plan

The team wrote an Experience Evaluation Plan that outlined methods that would be used to test our system.

The Experience Evaluation Plan included:

  • A lab study of student side testing
  • A lab study of home side testing
  • Materials needed to conduct testing
  • Personnel needed to conduct the test
  • Lab Study Procedure
  • Evaluation Criteria

Simple Evaluation Plan

The Simple Evaluation Plan was conducted in two phases:

  • Participants viewed a slide deck that introduced individual components of the system
  • A prototype video that demonstrated the system in use

Two students participated in the Simple Evaluation Plan. Upon completion, participants were interviewed about:

  • Overall impressions they had of our system
  • Safety concerns
  • Factors affecting system adoption and frequency of use
  • Potential benefits of using the system
  • Alternative system uses
