HomeConnect | User Experience Design

Overview

A Human Centered Design and Engineering team project that addresses the Chi 2012 design problem “Space, Place, Threshold: Considering the Experience of Home from Within and Without.”

The project focused on:

  • Research Methodology
  • Ideation
  • Sketching
  • Documentation
  • Presentations

Skills used: UX Research (Electronic Survey, Diary Study, Related Work Review;) Creating Personas; Sketching / Ideation; Documentation; Presentation.
