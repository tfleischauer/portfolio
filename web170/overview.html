Giddy Throng | WordPress, Web Design

Overview

This project was for an introductory WordPress course at Seattle Central College in summer 2014.

The class had the option of doing a personal site, or working with a client. I chose to work with Giddy Throng to gain more client-facing experience.

Giddy Throng is a business that buys collectible and vintage items, often refurbishes the items, then acts as a reseller.

The clients did not have an existing site and wanted a system to aggregate and present their products.

Skills used : Illustrator, Photoshop, HTML, CSS, Responsive Design, WordPress
