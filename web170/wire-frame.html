Wire Frame

This eight-week course allowed about a week to find a client, and to solidify the project concept.

Discovery exercises and wire frames were not a course requirement, but have become part of my usual work flow.

I made this quick wire frame in Adobe Illustrator to get the conversation started.

I also conducted abbreviated exercises with my client to identify who would be their anticipated users, and what these anticipated users needs might be.

My worst fear is spending time creating a system that no one will use.
