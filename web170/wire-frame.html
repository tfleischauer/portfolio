Wire Frame

This was an abbreviated and accelerated introductory WordPress course.

The course allowed about a week to find a client, and for discovery of the client’s goals and needs of the system’s users.

Wire frames were not a requirement but have become part of my usual work flow.

I chose to make this quick wire frame in Adobe Illustrator, to get the conversation started with my client.

I did additional unassigned discovery exercises where I had my client identify who his anticipated users are and to anticipate their needs (an abbreviated version of the discovery methodology used for the Nan Nalder piece.)

My worst fear is spending time creating a system that no one will use.
