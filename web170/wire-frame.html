Wire Frame

This eight-week course was more about learning WordPress than User-Experience Design. The course allowed about a week to find a client and for discovery of said client’s goals and needs of the system’s users.

Wire frames were not a requirement for the course, but have become part of my usual work flow. I chose to make this quick wire frame in Adobe Illustrator, to get the conversation started with my client.

I did additional unassigned discovery exercises where I had my client identify who his anticipated users are and to anticipate their needs (an abbreviated version of the discovery methodology used for the Nan Nalder piece.)

My worst fear is spending time creating a system that no one will use.
