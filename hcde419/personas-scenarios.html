Personas & Scenarios

Our Personas:

  • Mark the Musician
  • Paul the Producer
  • Lucy the Listener

Mark the Musician in Concrete, WA, Paul the Producer in New York, and Lucy the Listener use the system as a collaborative composition tool.

Lucy the Listener, is able to make comments on specific areas of Mark and Paul's audio tracks using a graphical interface that shows the audio files on a time line, or by posting general discussion comments, or by providing feedback using a system of sliders.

Although Lucy’s role is different than a producer or musician, she is a collaborator in the process.

Lucy provides perspective from an actual ‘user’ of the music, a potential fan.

Another scenario for users to express and develop ideas, is to create their own arrangement of the song's audio tracks via the 'Trigger List' editing tool.
