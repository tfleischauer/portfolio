Personas & Scenarios

The team created personas and scenarios to demonstrate how users would interact with our remote collaboration system.

Our Personas:

  • Mark the Musician (WA)
  • Paul the Producer (NY)
  • Lucy the Listener (CA)

Together these personas remotely use the system as a collaborative composition tool.

Example Scenario - A Listener

Lucy the Listener is not a musician or producer, yet she is still a valued collaborator. Listeners contribute a perspective from an actual ‘user’ of the developing music, a potential fan.

Lucy is able to contribute by:

  • Commenting on any audio track's time line
  • Providing feedback using a system of sliders
  • Posting general discussion comments
  • Commenting on Lyrics
