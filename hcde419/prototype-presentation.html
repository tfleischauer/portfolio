1st Prototype Presentation

With no constraints on the visual design, my team elected to individually design and present an aspect of the prototype that we felt was important.

I contributed a feature to allow remote collaborators to upload black and white documents that could be shown to both parties on a digital whiteboard (slides 4 - 7.)

The content of an uploaded document would be erasable, like a chalkboard eraser. Collaborators could use a mouse, stylus, or type directly into the whiteboard area to express their ideas.

This document-to-digital-whiteboard feature was based off research on how some collaborators write notes and pseudo charts in-person.

Simultaneously, the environment would allow video conferencing for better communication such as facial expressions and tone of voice. Textual chat would also be an option for users who desired a more private or asynchronous approach.
