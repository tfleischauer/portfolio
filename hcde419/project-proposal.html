Project Proposal

Our Human Centered Design and Engineering team decided to address the space of remote music collaboration.

Preparing for this presentation required the team to decide on a project concept, and the research methods needed to design the system.

Each group member individually prepared and presented a sequence of slides outlining our proposal.

The Project Proposal was a PechaKucha presentation (20 slides at a rate of 20 seconds per slide.)

I presented slides 6 through 10 that spoke about how musicians currently collaborate.
