Random Trombone | Human-Computer Interaction Concepts

Overview

A Human Centered Design and Engineering team project at the University of Washington that employs HCI research to design a remote collaboration environment for musicians.

HCI research methods included:

  • Academic Literature Reviews
  • User Interviews
  • Competitive Research

The process included:

  • Ideation
  • Sketching
  • Presentation
  • Documentation

Skills used: UX Research (Competitve Research, Literature Review;) design development; presentation; documentation; Adobe Illustrator.
