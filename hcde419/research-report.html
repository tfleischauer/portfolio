Research Report

Our team performed:

  • Competitive Research
  • Literature Reviews
  • User Interviews

I performed the Competitive Research, and the whole team performed Literature Reviews.

For the Competitive Research, I created accounts at existing music collaboration sites. I used the systems, and compared features.

One recurring discovery was that many collaborative sites were closer to social media sites than real-time collaboration environments. We were seeking to design a real-time collaboration environment.
