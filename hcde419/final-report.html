Final Report

Our Final Report:

  • Summarized our research methods and findings
  • Showed our design process (such as creating personas and scenarios, use of instructor and class feedback, iteration, and testing.)
  • Outlined and Documented our User Interface concepts

Our final project also included a video prototype. The prototype showed the interaction between our personas Mark the Musician in Concrete WA, and Paul the Producer in New York.

Mark the Musician and Paul the Producer use the digital whiteboard to work out differing conceptual details of how a chord sequence of their song should go.

We also used a third persona, Lucy the Listener, who used the system as a means of commentary on Mark and Paul’s evolving song. The graphical interface showed the audio files as a time line. Lucy was able to make comments on specific areas of their uploaded sound files.

Although, Lucy the Listener’s role was different than a producer or musician, she was ultimately a collaborator in the process. Lucy provided perspective from an actual ‘user’ of the music, a potential fan.
