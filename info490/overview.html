Client: Undergraduate Academic Affairs | Design Development Interaction Systems

Overview

This Information School group at the University of Washington recognized the difficulty for some students to make a major life decision in picking an academic major.

Our team designed a Web based interactive system, based on results from a RIASEC personality assessment, to help students to be better informed of their academic options at the University of Washington before declaring their academic major.

My contributions to this project:

  • Interviewed Academic Counselors
  • Interviewed UW Students
  • Administered Student Survey
  • Design Ideation and Iteration
  • Presentations to the Class
  • Production of Video Prototype
  • Presentation to the UW iSchool
