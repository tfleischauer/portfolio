Overview

Even though the official name was ART 483: Advanced Interaction Design, this UW course was more commonly known as 'Microsoft Expo.'

'Microsoft Expo' is a design competition for invited Interaction Design programs to showcase their ideas adhering to a theme.

This competition's theme was 'Information in my World.'

Our Team

Our IxD team focused attention to the aging population.

We wanted to design a system to keep seniors engaged in their interpersonal relationships both during and after their conversations.
