Process + Ideation

My greatest contribution to the project was during the concept development process.

I actively participated in:

  • Concept Ideation & Development Meetings
  • Writing Copy for Presentations & Documentation
  • Weekly Presentations

Each week, our process was a cycle of:

  • Incorporate Critique Feedback
  • Design Iteration
  • Prepare Artifacts for the Next Presentation

The most frequent ideation techniques used were:

  • Mind Mapping
  • Card Sorting
