Design

*MARK is a migrating application that acts as an audio filter that leverages hyperlinking with a system of tagging (or marking) parts of conversations that are too important to interrupt.

Marked conversation pieces can be referenced immediately or reviewed later.

Our team identified 'Dignity' as a key design component. To this end, *MARK is a non-intrusive means of bridging conversations.

My role on this team included influence on design decisions throughout the process of:

  • Ideation
  • Interviews
  • Presentations
